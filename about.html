Time is now, and time is all we have. Most people just exist. They go through life using words like practical and for them, today is just another day. A small percentage of people live. They realize today is your life. I love a comfy place and a book. Throw in a coffee and I’m a happy camper. Make it outside my favorite cafe in the sun with avocado toast and I couldn’t be happier. Some people know what I’m talking about. Instead of iPhones and green juice, give them magazines, sun, books, etc. The live slow and love the things they take time to surround themselves by, Clothes are intimate, which most brands don’t stress. You are careful with who gets to touch your body. Clothes are on your skin almost every waking moment. You should be careful, and excited, about the clothes against your skin. We wanted to make a garment that evoked and encouraged feeling and emotion. We wanted a guy to stop having to chose between a button down, a polo, and a cotton t-shirt. We wanted to bring beauty into the world. We tried hard. We hope you enjoy the result.
